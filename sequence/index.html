How to play

  • Goal: be first to get 5 chips in a row — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.
  • The four ★ corners are free spaces and count for everyone.
  • Tap a card in your hand to highlight the board spaces it can play. Tap a glowing space to drop a chip.
  • Each card matches two spaces on the board. After you play, you draw a new card.
  • Two-eyed Jacks (♦ ♣) are wild — place a chip on any empty space.
  • One-eyed Jacks (♠ ♥) remove one opponent chip (not one already in a completed sequence).
  • If both spaces for a card are full it's a dead card — tap it to swap it for a new one.
